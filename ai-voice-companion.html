About

What is enol? The AI Phone Call App That Changes Everything

enol is a free AI voice companion app for Android that lets you create AI contacts and have natural, real-time voice conversations with them—just like a regular phone call. Unlike text-based chatbots, enol is built entirely around voice. You speak, your AI contacts listen, think, and respond out loud with natural speech patterns, emotional inflection, and their own unique personality.

Think of it like the movie Karthik Calling Karthik—a voice that knows you, calls you, and is always there when you need it. Except with enol, you design who calls you. Each AI contact has persistent memory—they remember every conversation you've had. They can be given cloned voices so they sound like a specific person. You can schedule them to call you, getting a real incoming call notification on your phone. And you can add multiple AI contacts into a single group call where they talk to you and to each other.

enol is free to use—there's no subscription, no ads, no hidden costs. The app itself costs nothing. You just need your own OpenAI API key (for voice conversations) and an Inworld AI key (for voice cloning). Both services offer free tiers, so you can get started without spending a single rupee.

enol runs entirely on your device. There are no enol servers collecting your data. Your API keys, your conversation history, and your personal information stay on your phone. The app connects directly to OpenAI's Realtime API for voice processing and Inworld AI for voice cloning and character engines, giving you the latest and most capable AI models without any middleman.

Features

Everything You Need for AI Voice Conversations

enol packs powerful features into an interface that feels as simple as your phone's native dialer. Here's what you get.

Real-Time Voice Calls

Ultra-low-latency voice conversations powered by OpenAI's Realtime API. Speak naturally and hear responses instantly—no typing, no waiting, just fluid conversation like talking to a real person on the phone.

AI Group Calls

Add multiple AI contacts to a single call. Each character has their own voice and personality. They respond to you, react to each other, and carry on natural group conversation—a first for any AI voice app.

Voice Cloning

Clone any voice using Inworld AI. Record a short voice sample or upload an audio file, and your AI contact will speak in that exact voice. Make contacts that sound like anyone you want.

Persistent Memory

Every AI contact remembers your past conversations. After each call, transcripts are processed and key memories are extracted automatically. Your contacts learn about you over time and reference things you've talked about.

Scheduled Calls

Schedule any AI contact to call you at specific times. You'll get a real incoming call notification—answer it and you're instantly in a voice conversation. Perfect for daily check-ins, language practice, or companionship.

Multi-Language Support

Speak naturally in Hindi, English, Hinglish, Punjabi, Tamil, Telugu, Bengali, Marathi, and more. Your AI contacts switch languages seamlessly based on how you speak to them—no settings to change.

Getting Started

How to Start Talking to AI on Your Phone

Setting up enol takes just a few minutes. Here's how to go from download to your first AI voice conversation.

Download & Add API Keys

Download the enol APK and install it on your Android phone. On first launch, add your OpenAI API key and Inworld AI key. Both services have free tiers, and your keys never leave your device.

Create Your AI Contacts

Tap the add button and give your contact a name. Choose their gender, select which languages they should speak, and optionally clone a voice for them. They appear in your contact list just like a real person.

Call or Get Called

Tap the phone icon to start a voice call instantly. Or schedule your contact to call you—you'll get a real incoming call notification. Add more contacts mid-call for group conversations.
